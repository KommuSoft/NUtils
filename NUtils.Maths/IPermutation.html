IPermutation Interface

An interface representing a permutation. A permutation is permutable.

Syntax

public interface IPermutation : NUtils.Abstract.IResetable, IPermutable, ITransition, IEnumerable<int>

Remarks

A permutation is a special case of a NUtils.Maths.ITransition where two original indices cannot point to the same target index. It is in other words a bijective transition.
